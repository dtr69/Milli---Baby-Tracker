Privacy Policy (Milli Baby Monitor)

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

Milli Baby Monitor (“Milli,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) helps families track baby care activities. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the app:

  • Account information: email address and authentication data managed through Supabase Authentication.
  • Family and collaboration data: family name, family member email, role, title, invite codes, and join requests.
  • Baby care tracking data: logs you create, such as feeding, diaper, sleep, shower, vitamin, growth, medicine, and symptom/activity entries (for example temperature, medication, puke/diarrhea tracking when enabled).
  • Optional baby profile data: baby age (weeks/months) and gender, if you choose to enter it.
  • Notification data: device push token and platform (iOS/Android), plus reminder settings.
  • Local app data: cached logs and settings stored on your device (AsyncStorage) to support offline use and faster performance.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • provide core app features (tracking, history, insights, reminders);
  • sync your data across devices and family members in the same family group;
  • send family activity and reminder notifications;
  • support account and family management;
  • maintain app reliability and security.

3. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell personal information.

We may share data only as needed with:

  • Your family group members (based on family membership and permissions in the app).
  • Service providers that power app functionality:
    • Supabase (authentication and database),
    • Expo push services (delivery of push notifications).
  • Legal/compliance recipients where required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Notifications

If enabled, Milli may send:

  • local reminders on your device (feeding, diaper, sleep, bottle, medicine), and
  • push notifications to family members for shared activity updates.

You can disable notifications in app settings or device settings.

5. Data Retention

  • Cloud tracking/history data is retained until deleted by users (for example, deleting history or account-related data in settings).
  • Local device cache/settings remain until you clear app data, uninstall, or use in-app deletion/clear flows.
  • Some account-level auth records may be retained by the authentication provider unless separately removed under provider controls.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

  • update account email/password;
  • edit or delete tracking entries;
  • delete all family tracking history (owner role);
  • delete your account data from the app experience;
  • leave a family group;
  • disable notifications.

For privacy requests, including questions about account-level record deletion, contact us at: mobileapplicationplaysystems@gmail.com

7. Children’s Privacy

Milli is intended for use by parents/caregivers, not by children directly. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children as independent users.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

9. International Processing

Your information may be processed by service providers in different countries than your own.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” above shows the latest version date. Continued use of Milli after updates means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

Privacy questions and requests: mobileapplicationplaysystems@gmail.com
