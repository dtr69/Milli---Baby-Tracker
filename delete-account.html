Delete Your Account – Milli Baby Monitor

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data from Milli Baby Monitor.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Milli Baby Monitor app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by emailing: mobileapplicationplaysystems@gmail.com

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account email and authentication data
  • Baby tracking logs (feeding, sleep, diaper, medicine, growth, symptoms, etc.)
  • Family collaboration data and roles
  • Push notification tokens
  • Stored preferences linked to your account

Data Retention

Account deletion is processed immediately after confirmation. Some limited technical logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding your data, please contact: mobileapplicationplaysystems@gmail.com
